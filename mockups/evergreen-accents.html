Evergreen Chrome — Accent Options

Same Evergreen Chrome scheme (forest top bar & nav, calm light/dark bodies), with four replacements for the bright amber. The accent appears in three places: the favorite star, the active tab on the green chrome, and the celebrate banner.

Option A — All Green (no third color)

Cleanest

Drops the third color entirely: the star and celebrate use deep green (mint in dark mode), and the active tab is simply white against the forest chrome. Most restrained — the app is purely green, and nothing competes with the chrome.

Option B — Tomato Coral

Most energetic

A tomato-red coral — the classic complement to green, and very grocery (tomatoes, peppers). Warm and lively without being yellow; the light coral active tab pops clearly against the forest chrome.

Option C — Raspberry

Most distinctive

A berry pink-red — still produce-flavored (raspberries, beets) but more unexpected than coral. Reads a bit softer and more modern; strong contrast against the deep green in both modes.

Option D — Harvest Gold

Closest to current

If the problem is the brightness of #ffb52e rather than yellow itself: a deeper, muted ochre — wheat and honey rather than school-bus. Keeps the warm green-and-gold pairing but stops shouting.
